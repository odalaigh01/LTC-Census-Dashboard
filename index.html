About this Dashboard

This tool cross-references Little Traverse Conservancy spatial data with 2020 US Census Redistricting blocks. Adjust the buffer radius above and select a feature to calculate the total population living within its impact zone. The dashboard automatically mathematically merges overlapping buffers to calculate a true, deduplicated aggregate population reach.
